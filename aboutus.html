KingMill needs your help to solve his coding bugs!

Our aim

We want to teach young people between the ages of 10 and 15 years old how to code, but in a fun and interative way. Long gone are the days of reading lines of code and learning what the variables "x" and "i" mean. We want to teach by using real life examples which young people can easily understand, to make coding concepts easier to grasp.
